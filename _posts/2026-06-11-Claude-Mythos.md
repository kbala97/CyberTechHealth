Claude Mythos makes the need for threatmodeling even more critical: Providing best practice guidelines to product teams on architectural patterns and anti-patterns to secure your system by design. The old adage "Prevention is better than cure" applies here! Plus, the practice needs to be governed, auditable and fully traceable with some deterministic security requirements and countermeasures evaluted and implemented.
